Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 05, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you interact with our Facebook automation service.

1. Information We Collect

When you comment on our Facebook Page, we receive the following information via Facebook Webhooks:

  • Your Facebook User ID
  • Your public comment content
  • The timestamp of your comment

2. How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Automatically "Like" your comment.
  • Post a public reply to your comment.
  • Send you automated direct messages (DMs) with information you requested.

3. Data Retention

We do not store your user data permanently. It is processed in real-time to trigger the automation and then discarded from our server memory.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. All interactions happen within the Facebook ecosystem.

5. Your Rights

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying "STOP" or blocking our Facebook Page.

6. Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, please contact us via our Facebook Page.
